Discover Yourself in The Carpathians

Test your strength by conquering the peaks

Choose your route

Choose a peak that you have long wanted to visit and join a group of tourist. Our guides are experienced, they will not only help you with the overcoming the difficulties of the route, but also they will make the trip fun.

Learn interesting facts

Each mountain is special, they have their own history and legend. Our guides will tell you these facts and myths. Thanks to this, you will be able to immerse yourself even deeper in the mountain atmosphere.

It's more fun together

You can organize a group yourself, gathering your friends and relatives, and we will be happy to offer you the services of our guide. However, if your company is not large, you can join an existing tour group.

Our gear is your gear

Many experienced climbers already have their equipment tested by many routes. However, if you have only recently taken up mountaineering, or you have not been able to find any equipment, we can offer you the necessary equipment for rent.
